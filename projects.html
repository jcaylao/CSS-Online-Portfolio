Project Description

A grocery and cooking app that allows users to easily find new recipes and keep track of their groceries. One feature is recipe tinder, in which users are given a recipe and they can choose to like or dislike a recipe. Another feature is a receipt scanner that allows users to scan their receipts and upload it to their virtual pantry.
